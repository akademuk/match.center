Does everybody like accumulator bets? No

At first glance, a Parlay seems to be a good deal for the player. You can put a number of events together in one bet and increase your bankroll by several times. However, at a distance, express bets are more profitable for the betting site than for the player. The main reason for this is the bookmaker's margin.

Margin is the commission that is incurred in each betting market. The betting site takes this 2-10% from every bet won, at a distance of 90-98% instead of 100% of the fees collected.

It doesn't matter to the company whether the client wins or loses now. Every odds already has a profit in it. And if you bet a parlay, you pay that margin multiple times - from every bet you include in the accumulator.

How to manage the bankroll

On every betting website you will read about how important it is to manage your budget properly. But what does it mean?

We'll dispense with nerdy terms like "Kelly Criterion" or "Martingale System", especially as these approaches don't work for most people.

Our approach works. The rule is very simple: allocate a specific amount of money within your entertainment budget for sports betting. If you win, great. But if you lose everything you've allocated, that's the end of the story. Wait for next week or month - depending on what period you're planning for.

Betting is an intellectual hobby, but still it is a gambling hobby. And in order to control your bankroll, it is important to know how to:

  • Manage your emotions;
  • Choose best betting sites;
  • Stick to a system when making bets;
  • Understand the sports you are betting on.
Odds and markets comparison

A good way to find favourable odds is to register on several online betting websites. Very often there are situations where betting sites estimate the probability of the outcome of the same event differently. This means that someone may have higher odds on the same betting markets.

For example, William Hill and Betway put different odds on a draw in an English Premier League match between Crystal Palace and Leeds United.

On the one hand, the difference in odds is only 1/10. However, at the distance, this painstaking approach to selecting a betting site will pay off handsomely. And not just because the payouts will be bigger. But also because a methodical comparison is a great way to resist impulse betting.
